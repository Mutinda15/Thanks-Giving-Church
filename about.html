About Us

We are a community of believers dedicated to spreading the love of Jesus Christ and helping others grow in faith. Our mission is to offer a warm welcoming environment where all are welcome to come and learn more about our Lord and Savior.

Services

We offer a variety of services each week, including Sunday morning worship, mid-week Bible studies, and youth mentorship programs. Join us as we come together to celebrate the love and grace of God.

Events

We have a number of exciting events coming up in the next few months, including extentional worship, choir retreat, and a concert by a local Christian artist. Be sure to check back here for updates and more information about these and other events.
